Privacy Policy — Luna Log

Last updated: March 24, 2026

Overview

Luna Log is a private period and symptom tracking app. Your health data stays on your device. We collect nothing.

Data Collection

We collect no data. None.

  • No account required
  • No network requests
  • No analytics or telemetry
  • No advertising SDKs
  • No third-party services

Data Storage

All data (period logs, symptoms, basal body temperature, notes, and settings) is stored exclusively in a local SQLite database on your device. The database is sandboxed inside the app's private storage and is not accessible to other apps.

Local Crash Logs

When the app crashes, a crash report is saved to the app's private internal storage on your device. These files contain only the error stack trace and a timestamp — no personal health data. A maximum of 10 crash logs are kept; older ones are automatically deleted. Crash logs are never sent anywhere. They remain on your device and are only accessible to you.

Data Sharing

Your data is never shared with anyone because it never leaves your device. The export feature (CSV/ZIP) creates a file locally using Android's Storage Access Framework (SAF) — you choose where to save or share the file. We have no involvement in or visibility into that process.

Backup & Restore

Luna Log supports local backup and restore via Android's Storage Access Framework (SAF). When you create a backup, the app writes a file to a location you choose on your device. When you restore, you select a backup file from your device. All backup operations are user-initiated and local — no cloud service is involved.

On-Device AI (Gemini Nano)

On supported devices (Pixel 8+ / Galaxy S24+), Luna Log may offer optional AI-powered insights using Google's Gemini Nano model. This model runs entirely on your device — no data is sent to Google, to any server, or off your device in any way. The feature is disabled by default and only activates on hardware that supports it.

Biometric / App Lock

If you enable App Lock, authentication is handled entirely by Android's BiometricManager using your device's biometric hardware (fingerprint, face, or PIN). No biometric data is accessed or stored by Luna Log.

Children

Luna Log is not directed at children under 13. We have no way to collect data from anyone, of any age.

Changes to This Policy
